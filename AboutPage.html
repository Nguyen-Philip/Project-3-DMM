What is Wellness In a Minute?

Wellness In a Minute is a short podcast that talks about solutions of being healthy as well as discussing problems that is plaguing people's health. The target audience is for those who are starting to try to get into a healthy lifestyle and have no idea when to start. Each session being a minute also allows it to fit perfectly into someone's schedule as it only takes a minute of your time.


History of Wellness In a Minute

Wellness In a Minute is a podcast started by Philip Nguyen to talk about health in 2023. Wellness In a Minute was a project for his Digital Multimedia class. The idea was generated when Philip Nguyen used ChatGPT to give him an idea on what type of audio project he should work on and it listed many different ideas and the one that stood out was a small podcast that talks about health in a minute.
